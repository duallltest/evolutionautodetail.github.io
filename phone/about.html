IT'S ALL ABOUT HAPPINESS

There was a time in my life I didn't see my purpose. It was before I worked out. I didn't believe in myself, all I focused on was my mistakes. I wasn't ever happy, always stressed etc over trauma in my life. One day I decided I was tired of feeling insecure and in-confident about my look and myself so I decided to workout. Now transforming my body did not happen over night. It has happened over 4 years of trial and error, of failure, and hurt but it made me strong. It forced me to set a goal and believe in it. Once, I finally saw how much of in control I had of my success, I took advantage and succeeded.. and grew confidence. I grew happiness. I grew into the person I am today and I would not change any of the hurt, any mistakes, and successes, because it's all apart of my journey to success. The SAME exact thing falls down for you. So take advantage every day of your ability to get up and strive to become who you want to be.
